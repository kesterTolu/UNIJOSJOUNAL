Editorial

The world is increasingly becoming a global village with the globalization of the economy, diversification of culture, increased competition, and information explosion. Within this scenario, investment in knowledge and knowledge-based systems becomes absolutely necessary in order to optimize and sustain development in any country. Education is without doubt the propeller of national development and one of the most viable vehicles of socio-economic transformation and development.

UJOSCE is a journal of the Faculty of Education, University of Jos. It is a medium for dialogue and sharing of ideas between academics and authors in Nigeria and beyond. The main target group includes university communities, educational organizations, and development partners.

Prof. Muraina Olawale Ojuola

Notes to Contributors

Well-researched articles and papers are invited from scholars in the fields of Education, Arts, Social Sciences, Science, and others for publication. The articles/papers may be products of descriptive research, analytical research, research reports, book reviews, and so on.

Submission Guidelines
  1. Manuscripts should be submitted either in hard copy or electronically as email attachment to the Journal of Contemporary Studies in Education.
  2. Each paper to be submitted must be original and not previously published or submitted for consideration elsewhere.
  3. All articles must be typewritten in A4 size using Times New Roman, double line spacing, 12 font size, and not more than 14 pages including references and appendices.
  4. Each paper must have an abstract of not more than 250 words, name of author(s), title of author(s), institutional affiliation(s), author’s(s’) phone number(s), and e-mail address(es).
  5. Current APA referencing style must be strictly adhered to (both in the references and within the text).
