About UniJos

A Hub of Academic Excellence

Established as one of Nigeria's top academic institutions, the University of Jos (UniJos) continues to set the standard for education and research. Known for producing top scholars and cutting-edge research, UniJos is dedicated to fostering academic growth and making knowledge accessible to everyone.

Our online journal repository provides access to groundbreaking research from faculty and students, ensuring the world benefits from the knowledge produced within our walls.
